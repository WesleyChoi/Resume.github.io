About Me

Hey!

I'm Wesley and I'm currently a second year student at UBC in the faculty of Sciences. I would describe myself as a keen and curious individual - I love to code, make vector designs, and am super interested UX/UI design. I'm always eager to learn more about the world around me, give back to my community, and tackling difficult problems. I've been involved in graphic design for as long as I can remember; I've featured some of my past works and linked my portfolio below, so feel free to have a look!

Outside of school and work, you can find me working out, playing Ultimate Frisbee, playing the piano, and soaking up the rainy outdoors of Vancouver, B.C.!
